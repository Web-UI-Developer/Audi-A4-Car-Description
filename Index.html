Audi A4

The Audi A4 is a line of compact executive cars produced since late 1994 by the German car manufacturer Audi, a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group.The Audi A4 automobile layout consists of a longitudinal engine front-engine design, with transaxle-type transmissions mounted at the rear of the engine. The cars are front-wheel drive, "quattro" all-wheel drive.The A4 is available as a sedan, station wagon, covertable.

Interior Features

The 2015 Audi A4 comes standard with tri-zone automatic climate control, a 10-speaker audio system, Bluetooth phone connectivity, satellite radio, a power sunroof, an auxiliary audio input, an SD card slot, an iPod connector and Audi's MMI infotainment system with a 6.5-inch color display screen and a central control knob. Optional features include a rearview camera, Bluetooth audio streaming, adaptive cruise control, blind spot monitoring, HD Radio, rear parking sensors, a 14-speaker Bang & Olufsen audio system and a 7-inch MMI infotainment system with navigation.
